<p><strong>The Isotropic Graphite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIP Method</li><li>Vibration Molding Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isotropic Graphite Market consists of different types such as CIP Method and Vibration Molding Method. The CIP Method involves the pressing of graphite powder into a mold under high pressure, resulting in a high-density and uniform graphite structure. On the other hand, the Vibration Molding Method uses vibrations to compact the graphite powder in a mold, leading to a more consistent and homogenous graphite material. Both methods are commonly utilized in the production of high-quality isotropic graphite products for various industrial applications.</p></p>
