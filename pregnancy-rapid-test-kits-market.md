<p><p>Pregnancy rapid test kits come in two main types: line-indicator devices and digital devices. Line-indicator devices require the user to observe the appearance or absence of lines on a test strip to determine pregnancy results. On the other hand, digital devices display clear 'pregnant' or 'not pregnant' results on a digital screen, making it easier for users to interpret. Both types of kits are commonly used by individuals looking to quickly and conveniently confirm pregnancy at home.</p></p>
