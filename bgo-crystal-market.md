<p><ul><li>Czochralski Method</li><li>Bridgman Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BGO crystal market is primarily divided into two types based on the methods used for crystal growth: the Czochralski method and the Bridgman method. </p><p>The Czochralski method involves slowly pulling a seed crystal out of a molten solution to create a pure, single crystal structure. </p><p>On the other hand, the Bridgman method involves solidifying a crystal by slowly moving a crucible containing the molten material through a temperature gradient. </p><p>Both methods have their own advantages and applications in the BGO crystal market.</p></p>
