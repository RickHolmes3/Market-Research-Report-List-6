<p><ul><li>Vertical Spring Probes</li><li>Cantilever Spring Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS Spring Probes come in two main types - Vertical Spring Probes and Cantilever Spring Probes. Vertical Spring Probes are designed with a spring mechanism that allows for vertical compression and contact with the target surface. Cantilever Spring Probes, on the other hand, use a cantilever beam design to provide flexibility and contact with the target. Both types of probes are used in various applications such as semiconductor testing, PCB testing, and MEMS device testing due to their high sensitivity and reliability.</p></p>
