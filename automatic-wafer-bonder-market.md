<p><p>Automatic wafer bonder market is segmented into semi-automated wafer bonder and fully-automated wafer bonder. Semi-automated wafer bonders require some manual intervention during the bonding process, making them suitable for low to medium volume production. On the other hand, fully-automated wafer bonders operate without human intervention, maximizing efficiency and throughput for high volume production. Both types of bonders offer precise alignment and bonding of semiconductor wafers, but the level of automation varies to cater to different production needs.</p></p>
