<p><p>The wall-mounted rimless toilets market can be categorized into two main types: monolithic and separate. Monolithic models feature a seamless design where the toilet and wall-hung frame are integrated, providing a sleek, contemporary look while simplifying installation. In contrast, separate models consist of a wall-hung toilet installed independently from the flushing mechanism. This allows for greater flexibility in design and easier maintenance but may require more careful consideration of plumbing and support structures. Both types offer hygienic advantages with their rimless designs.</p></p>
