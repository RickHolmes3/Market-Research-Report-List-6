<p><ul><li>Permanent Packers</li><li>Retrievable Packers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent Packers are used in well completions to isolate different zones in the wellbore permanently. They are typically cemented in place and cannot be removed once set. Retrievable Packers, on the other hand, are used in situations where temporary isolation is required. They can be easily removed from the wellbore after their intended use. Both types of Packers play a crucial role in oil and gas operations by ensuring proper zonal isolation and well integrity.</p></p>
