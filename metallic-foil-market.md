<p><strong>The Metallic Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Stamping Foil</li><li>Cold Stamping Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic foil market can be categorized into two types: hot stamping foil and cold stamping foil. Hot stamping foil is applied using heat and pressure onto a material, creating a shiny and metallic finish. It is commonly used for packaging, labels, and printing. Cold stamping foil, on the other hand, does not require heat to apply and is often used for applications where heat-sensitive materials are involved. Both types of foils offer a wide range of color options and are popular choices in the packaging and printing industries.</p></p>
