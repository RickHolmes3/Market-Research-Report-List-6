<p><p>Terrestrial laser scanners are used for advanced 3D mapping and modeling applications. There are two main types of terrestrial laser scanners: Phase Based Scanners and Time-Of-Flight Scanners. Phase Based Scanners use the interference pattern between emitted and reflected laser light to determine distance measurements, while Time-Of-Flight Scanners measure the time it takes for a laser pulse to travel to a target and back. Both types of scanners are essential tools for various industries like construction, engineering, and archaeology for accurate and detailed data acquisition.</p></p>
