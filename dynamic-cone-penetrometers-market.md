<p><p>Dynamic Cone Penetrometers (DCP) are devices used to measure the penetration resistance of soils. There are two main types in the market- Single-Mass DCP and Dual-Mass DCP. </p><p>Single-Mass DCPs utilize a single mass system for impacting the soil, while Dual-Mass DCPs use two masses to provide a more controlled impact force. Single-Mass DCPs are simpler and more affordable, while Dual-Mass DCPs offer more precision and accuracy in measuring soil compaction. Both types are widely used in construction, geotechnical engineering, and agriculture.</p></p>
