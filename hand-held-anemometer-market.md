Cup anemometers measure wind speed by counting rotations of cups, vane anemometers measure wind direction using moving vanes, hot-wire anemometers use a heated wire to measure air speed, laser Doppler anemometers use laser beams to measure air speed, ultrasonic anemometers use sound waves to measure wind speed, while other types may utilize different technologies for measuring wind characteristics.</p></p>
