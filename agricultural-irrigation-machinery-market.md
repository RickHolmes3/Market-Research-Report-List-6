<p><ul><li>Sprinkler Irrigation</li><li>Drip Irrigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural irrigation machinery market primarily consists of two main types: sprinkler irrigation and drip irrigation. Sprinkler irrigation utilizes systems that spray water over crops, mimicking natural rainfall, making it suitable for various terrains. Drip irrigation, on the other hand, delivers water directly to the plant's root zone through a network of tubes and emitters, promoting efficient water use and reducing evaporation. Both methods enhance crop yield and resource efficiency, catering to diverse agricultural needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
