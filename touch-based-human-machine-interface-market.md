<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Capacitive</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touch based human machine interface market is divided into various types like resistive, capacitive, surface acoustic wave, and others. Resistive touchscreens consist of two layers separated by a small gap which registers touch by pressure. Capacitive touchscreens use electrical signals to sense touch. Surface acoustic wave touchscreens use ultrasonic waves to detect touch. Other types may include optical, infrared, and acoustic pulse recognition technologies. Each type offers unique functionalities and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
