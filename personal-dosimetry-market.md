<p><p>Personal dosimetry market is segmented into different types including Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD), Optically Stimulated Luminescence Dosimeters (OSL), Radiophotoluminescent (RPL) Dosimeters, and Active Dosimeters. TLD dosimeters use crystals that emit light when exposed to radiation, while OSL dosimeters measure radiation exposure using light emissions from a phosphor material. RPL dosimeters utilize fluorescent materials to detect radiation levels. Active dosimeters continuously monitor radiation levels in real time. Each type offers unique features and benefits for monitoring personal radiation exposure in various industries.</p></p>
