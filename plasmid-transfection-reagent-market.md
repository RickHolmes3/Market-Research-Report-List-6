<p><p>Plasmid transfection reagent market is segmented into Liposomal Based Reagents and Non-liposomal Based Reagents. Liposomal based reagents are lipid-based delivery systems that encapsulate the plasmid DNA, ensuring efficient transfection into the target cells. On the other hand, Non-liposomal based reagents include polymer-based or chemical-based transfection reagents that facilitate the transfer of plasmid DNA into cells without the use of lipids. Both types of reagents play a crucial role in genetic engineering and research applications.</p></p>
