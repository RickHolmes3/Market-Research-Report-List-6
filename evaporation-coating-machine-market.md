<p><p>Evaporation coating machines are utilized in the market for two main types of coating processes - Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD involves using a vacuum environment to evaporate a solid material and deposit it onto a substrate, while CVD involves chemical reactions to form a thin film on the substrate surface. Both processes are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and aerospace for applications like protective coatings, decorative finishes, and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
