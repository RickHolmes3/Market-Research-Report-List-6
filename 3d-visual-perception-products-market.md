<p><p>Structured light, iToF, dToF, Lidar, and other 3D visual perception products are innovative technologies used for depth sensing and spatial mapping. Structured light uses patterns of light to create 3D models, iToF measures depth based on the time-of-flight of light, dToF uses distance as a factor, and Lidar uses lasers for accurate depth detection. Other technologies in this market include stereo vision and time-of-flight cameras. These products cater to various industries such as robotics, automotive, healthcare, and entertainment.</p></p>
