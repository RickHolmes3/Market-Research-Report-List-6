<p><strong>The Membrane Cleaning Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Caustic</li><li>Oxidants or Disinfectants</li><li>Acids</li><li>Surfactants</li><li>Chelating Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The membrane cleaning chemicals market comprises several key types, each serving distinct purposes. Caustics, such as sodium hydroxide, are used to remove organic foulants. Oxidants or disinfectants, like chlorine and hydrogen peroxide, help eliminate microbial contaminants. Acids, such as hydrochloric acid, effectively dissolve mineral scale. Surfactants reduce surface tension, enhancing the removal of foulants, while chelating agents bind metals and prevent scale formation. Together, these chemicals improve membrane efficiency and prolong operational life in various applications.</p></p>
