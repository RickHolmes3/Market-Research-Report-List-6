<p><strong>The Monocrystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lifting Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monocrystal furnace market includes lifting furnace and floating zone furnace. Lifting furnace has a chamber that can be raised or lowered to control temperature and crystal growth. It is commonly used for producing large single crystals. Floating zone furnace uses electromagnetic induction to create a molten zone that moves along the crystal rod, allowing for precise control over crystal structure and purity. It is often used for growing crystals with high quality and minimal impurities, making it suitable for advanced applications.</p></p>
