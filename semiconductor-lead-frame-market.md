<p><p>The semiconductor lead frame market consists of two primary types: stamping process lead frames and etching process lead frames. Stamping process lead frames are produced through mechanical stamping, offering high efficiency and precision for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, etching process lead frames involve chemical etching techniques to create intricate designs and finer features, suitable for specialized applications. Both methods cater to the evolving demands of the semiconductor industry, supporting the miniaturization and performance enhancement of electronic components.</p></p>
