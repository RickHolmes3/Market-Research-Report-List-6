<p><p>Bi-Directional Couplers are categorized based on their coupling values, which represent the amount of signal that is transmitted or coupled from one port to another. Up to 10 dB couplers provide low coupling values, making them suitable for applications where minimal signal loss is required. 10 to 20 dB couplers offer moderate signal coupling, striking a balance between signal loss and signal transfer. 20 to 30 dB couplers provide high coupling values, enabling efficient signal transfer over longer distances. Each type caters to specific needs depending on the desired signal strength and loss.</p></p>
