<p><p>Pallet rack systems come in three main types: manual, semi-automated, and fully automated. Manual systems require workers to physically move and organize pallets on the racks. Semi-automated systems utilize technology to assist workers in tasks such as loading and unloading pallets. Fully automated systems use advanced technology to handle the entire process of storing and retrieving pallets without human intervention. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency, speed, and labor requirements to meet different storage and handling needs.</p></p>
