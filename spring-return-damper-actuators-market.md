<p><p>Spring return damper actuators are used in HVAC systems for controlling the flow of air. There are three main types of control in the market: Two-Position Control, Three-Position Control, and Modulating Control. </p><p>Two-Position Control actuators have two positions - open and closed. Three-Position Control actuators have three positions - fully open, fully closed, and a mid-position. Modulating Control actuators continuously adjust the position of the damper to regulate airflow precisely.</p><p>Each type of control offers different levels of precision and control in HVAC systems.</p></p>
