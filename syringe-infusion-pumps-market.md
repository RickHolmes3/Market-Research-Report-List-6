<p><p>Syringe infusion pumps are categorized into single-channel and dual-channel types, each serving distinct patient needs. Single-channel pumps administer medication through one syringe, offering simplicity and ease of use, ideal for patients requiring a single drug. In contrast, dual-channel pumps can deliver two different medications simultaneously, enhancing treatment efficiency for complex cases. Both types are essential in clinical settings, ensuring precise drug delivery, minimizing human error, and improving overall patient care and outcomes in various medical scenarios.</p></p>
