<p><strong>The Fibre Laser Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Fibre Laser</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fibre laser machines are categorized into two main types: pulsed fibre lasers and continuous wave (CW) fibre lasers. Pulsed fibre lasers emit high-power laser beams in short pulses, making them ideal for cutting, welding, and marking materials. On the other hand, CW fibre lasers produce a continuous beam of high-intensity light that is well-suited for applications such as laser cutting and engraving. Both types of fibre lasers have their own advantages and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
