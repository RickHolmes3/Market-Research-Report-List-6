Type B packaging consists of flexible materials such as pouches and wraps, suitable for low to moderate-risk devices. Other types of packaging include trays, clamshells, and thermoformed packaging. Each type of packaging is designed to maintain sterility and protect medical devices from contamination, ensuring safety and efficacy for patients.</p></p>
