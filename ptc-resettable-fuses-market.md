<p><p>The PTC resettable fuses market comprises primarily two types: CPTC (Cold PTC) and PPTC (Polymeric PTC). CPTC devices operate effectively at lower temperatures, while PPTC fuses are designed for higher thermal thresholds. Both types serve essential roles in protecting electronic circuits from overcurrent conditions by self-resetting after cooling down. Their applications span consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, where they enhance safety and reliability while minimizing the need for manual intervention in circuit protection.</p></p>
