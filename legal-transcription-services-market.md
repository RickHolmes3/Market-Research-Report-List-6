Depositions transcription pertains to spoken testimonies recorded outside the courtroom. Interrogations transcription involves the transcription of questions and answers during legal interviews. Hearings transcription entails converting courtroom proceedings into written documents. Other market types in legal transcription services may include legal briefs, judgments, and correspondence.</p></p>
