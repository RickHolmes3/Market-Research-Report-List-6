Single pendulum systems utilize a single arm that sweeps across the windscreen, offering a simplified design and efficient wiping action. In contrast, dual pendulum systems employ two arms for enhanced coverage and improved wiping performance, particularly on larger windscreens. These systems are crucial for maintaining visibility and safety in rail transport, catering to varying demands of different train models and operational environments.</p></p>
