<p><ul><li>Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li><li>Non-Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof fabrics market comprises two main types: breathable and non-breathable fabrics. Breathable waterproof fabrics allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing water ingress, making them ideal for outdoor gear and active apparel, enhancing comfort during physical activities. In contrast, non-breathable waterproof fabrics offer a complete barrier against water but can trap moisture, leading to discomfort. These are commonly used in applications like raincoats and tarps where water protection is paramount, but breathability is less critical.</p></p>
