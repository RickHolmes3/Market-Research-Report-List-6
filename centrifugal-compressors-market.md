<p><ul><li>Single-stage Centrifugal Compressors</li><li>Multi-stage Centrifugal Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal compressors are categorized into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage centrifugal compressors utilize one impeller to achieve pressure increase, ideal for low-pressure applications and offering simplicity and lower costs. In contrast, multi-stage centrifugal compressors incorporate multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure ratios and efficiency, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes. Both types serve various sectors, including power generation, petrochemicals, and refrigeration, with specific designs tailored to meet the diverse operational requirements of different industries.</p></p>
