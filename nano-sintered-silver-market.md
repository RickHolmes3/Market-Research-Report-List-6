<p><p>Nano sintered silver is used in two main market types: Pressure Sinter and Non-Pressure Sinter. In Pressure Sinter, high pressure is applied to the silver particles to force them to bond together, resulting in a dense and durable material. This method is commonly used in electronic packaging and thermal interface materials. In Non-Pressure Sinter, the silver particles are sintered at lower pressures, making it suitable for applications where gentle bonding is required, such as in flexible electronics and medical devices.</p></p>
