The market includes various types of sensors such as Activity Sensor, Heart Rate Variability Sensor, Minute Ventilation Sensor, Intra-Thoracic Impedance, and Implantable Hemodynamic Monitors. Activity sensors track physical movements, heart rate variability sensors measure changes in heart rate, minute ventilation sensors monitor breathing, intra-thoracic impedance sensors measure changes in thoracic cavity fluid levels, and implantable hemodynamic monitors provide continuous monitoring of cardiovascular parameters. These sensors play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
