<p><ul><li>Band Pass Filter</li><li>Long Pass Filter</li><li>Short Pass Filter</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone camera filters market includes various types, each serving specific functions. Band pass filters allow certain wavelengths to pass while blocking others, enhancing color accuracy. Long pass filters permit longer wavelengths, often improving low-light performance. Short pass filters, on the other hand, block longer wavelengths, enhancing clarity for specific applications. Other market segments may include specialized filters like UV or polarization filters, which are used to reduce glare and improve image quality in diverse shooting conditions.</p></p>
