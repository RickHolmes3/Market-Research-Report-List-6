<p><p>Solar panels are categorized into two main types: Crystalline Silicon Solar Panels and Thin-Film Solar Panels. Crystalline Silicon Solar Panels consist of silicon crystals that are arranged in a precise structure, making them highly efficient and reliable. On the other hand, Thin-Film Solar Panels are made by depositing a thin layer of photovoltaic material onto a substrate. While they are more flexible and lightweight, they tend to be less efficient. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, catering to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
