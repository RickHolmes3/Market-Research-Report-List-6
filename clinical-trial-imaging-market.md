<p><p>Clinical trial imaging services are typically categorized into two main types: in-house imaging services and central imaging services. In-house imaging services involve utilizing equipment and resources within the clinical trial site to conduct imaging procedures on study participants. On the other hand, central imaging services involve outsourcing the imaging procedures to specialized central imaging facilities, which often provide more advanced technology and expertise. Both types of imaging services play a crucial role in assisting researchers to collect and analyze medical imaging data for clinical trials.</p></p>
