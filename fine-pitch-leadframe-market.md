<p><p>Fine pitch leadframes are used in various electronic devices for connecting semiconductor chips to external circuits. Two common types of fine pitch leadframes are stamping process leadframe and etching process leadframe. Stamping process leadframes are manufactured by stamping out metal sheets to form the desired shape and pattern. Etching process leadframes, on the other hand, are produced by chemically etching metal sheets to create intricate designs with high precision. Both types of leadframes are crucial components in the semiconductor industry for ensuring reliable connections and high performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
