<p><ul><li>Mechanical Jacquard looms</li><li>Electronic Jacquard looms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jacquard loom market consists of two primary types: mechanical and electronic Jacquard looms. Mechanical Jacquard looms use traditional methods with punched cards to control the weaving process, offering reliability and low maintenance. In contrast, electronic Jacquard looms utilize digital technology, providing enhanced flexibility, precision, and the ability to produce complex designs efficiently. The electronic variant is increasingly preferred for modern textile manufacturing due to its speed and versatility, catering to the evolving demands of the fashion and home textile industries.</p></p>
