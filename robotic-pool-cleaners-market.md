<p><ul><li>Crawler Drive</li><li>Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crawler Drive robotic pool cleaners move on tracks to navigate the pool and clean efficiently. These types of cleaners are known for their superior traction and ability to clean tough surfaces, such as stairs and walls. On the other hand, Wheel Drive robotic pool cleaners use wheels to move around the pool, providing a smooth and consistent cleaning performance. While both types are effective in cleaning pools, Crawler Drive cleaners are typically preferred for larger or irregularly shaped pools, while Wheel Drive cleaners are popular for smaller, simpler pool layouts.</p></p>
