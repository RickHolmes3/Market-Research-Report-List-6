<p><p>Coreless DC motors are specialized devices known for their efficiency and compact design, primarily categorized into cylindrical and disc motors. Cylindrical coreless motors feature a tubular structure ideal for applications requiring high torque and low inertia, making them suitable for robotics and automation. In contrast, disc coreless motors offer a flat design, optimizing space and weight in compact applications such as portable electronics and medical devices. Both types leverage a coreless winding structure for enhanced performance and reduced electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
