<p><p>ECG telemetry devices market can be categorized into three main types: Resting ECG devices, Stress ECG devices, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG devices are used for routine monitoring of heart activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG devices are used during physical stress tests to monitor the heart's response to exercise. Holter Monitors are portable devices worn by patients for 24-48 hours to continuously monitor heart activity during normal daily activities. These devices help healthcare professionals diagnose various heart conditions and monitor treatment effectiveness.</p></p>
