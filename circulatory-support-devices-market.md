<p><ul><li>Ventricular Assisted Device (VAD)</li><li>Total Artificial Heart (TAH)</li><li>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Circulatory support devices are utilized to assist heart function in patients with heart failure. Ventricular Assisted Devices (VAD) help the heart pump blood throughout the body, while Total Artificial Hearts (TAH) completely replace the heart's functions. Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) provides temporary support by oxygenating the blood outside the body. These devices play a crucial role in managing heart failure patients and improving their overall quality of life. The market for these devices continues to grow as the demand for advanced circulatory support options increases.</p></p>
