<p><p>Semiconductor strain gauge sensors are used in various industries to measure strain, pressure, and force. There are two main types of semiconductor strain gauge sensors: Naked Gages and Backed Gages. Naked Gages are exposed to the environment and directly measure the strain on the material. Backed Gages, on the other hand, are mounted onto a backing material for protection and improved durability. Both types of sensors are widely used in applications where precise measurement of strain and pressure is required.</p></p>
