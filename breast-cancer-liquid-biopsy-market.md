<p><p>The breast cancer liquid biopsy market includes various types of circulating biomarkers used for non-invasive cancer diagnosis and monitoring. Circulating Tumor Cells (CTCs) are cancer cells shed into the bloodstream, providing insights into tumor characteristics. Cell-Free DNA (CfDNA) consists of fragments of DNA released from dying tumor cells, aiding in genetic analysis. Extracellular Vesicles (EVs) are membrane-bound particles that carry proteins and nucleic acids, contributing to cancer signaling. Other circulating biomarkers include various substances that may indicate cancer presence or progression.</p></p>
