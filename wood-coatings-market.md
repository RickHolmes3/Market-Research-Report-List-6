<p><p>The wood coatings market is primarily categorized into oil-based, water-based, and solvent-based types. Oil-based coatings provide durability and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, quick-drying, and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. Solvent-based coatings offer excellent adhesion and a smooth finish, suitable for professional applications. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application ease in the wood finishing industry.</p></p>
