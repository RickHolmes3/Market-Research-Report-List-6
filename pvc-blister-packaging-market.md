<p><strong>The PVC Blister Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC blister packaging is commonly available in two main types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating a PVC sheet until it becomes pliable, then forming it into the desired shape using a mold. This method is ideal for large-scale production of uniform shapes. On the other hand, cold forming does not require heat and involves stretching the PVC sheet over a mold using pressure. This method is preferred for packaging products that are sensitive to heat. Both methods offer durable and secure packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
