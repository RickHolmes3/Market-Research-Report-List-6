<p><p>The H-Bridge driver market is segmented into Single H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge types. A Single H-Bridge controls one motor, allowing for bidirectional movement and speed control, making it ideal for compact applications. In contrast, a Dual H-Bridge can control two motors independently, offering more versatility and efficiency in larger systems like robotics or automation. Both types are vital in various applications, including electronics, automotive, and industrial machinery, catering to different power and control requirements.</p></p>
