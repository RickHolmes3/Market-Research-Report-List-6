<p><p>Vacuum tube solar collectors are designed to harness solar energy efficiently, primarily categorized into two market types: thermal oil type and molten salt type. The thermal oil type uses heat transfer oils to absorb solar energy, which is then utilized for heating applications, particularly in industrial settings. In contrast, the molten salt type employs a mixture of salts that can operate at higher temperatures, enabling energy storage and dispatch capabilities, making it suitable for utility-scale solar power generation. Both types enhance energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
