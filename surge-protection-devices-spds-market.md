<p><strong>The Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) are classified into three main types based on their operation: Voltage Switch Type SPD, Pressure Limiting Type SPD, and Combination Type SPD. Voltage Switch Type SPDs divert excess voltage by switching in response to a surge. Pressure Limiting Type SPDs limit the voltage level by absorbing energy through gas discharge tubes or metal oxide varistors. Combination Type SPDs combine both technologies for added protection. Each type caters to different environments and requirements, providing a comprehensive solution for surge protection in various applications.</p></p>
