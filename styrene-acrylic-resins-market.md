<p><strong>The Styrene Acrylic Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene acrylic resins are categorized into two primary markets: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based resins utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean, while providing strong adhesion and durability in coatings and adhesives. Solvent-based resins, on the other hand, use organic solvents, offering excellent performance in terms of gloss, abrasion resistance, and weatherability, though they may be less eco-friendly. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries like construction, automotive, and coatings.</p></p>
