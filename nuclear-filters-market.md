<p><p>Nuclear filters market primarily consists of HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and ULPA (Ultra Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filters are designed to remove 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns in size, while ULPA filters have an even higher efficiency, removing 99.999% of particles down to 0.12 microns. Other filters in the market include charcoal filters, bag filters, and cartridge filters, each serving a specific purpose in maintaining air quality and safety in nuclear facilities.</p></p>
