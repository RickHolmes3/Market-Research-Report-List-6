<p><p>Composites market types include layup, filament winding, injection molding, pultrusion, and others. Layup involves placing layers of reinforcement material onto a mold in a specific sequence. Filament winding uses continuous fibers wound around a rotating mandrel. Injection molding involves injecting resin into a mold cavity. Pultrusion is a continuous process of manufacturing composite profiles with fibers pulled through a resin bath. Other methods include compression molding, resin transfer molding, and vacuum infusion. Each method offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
