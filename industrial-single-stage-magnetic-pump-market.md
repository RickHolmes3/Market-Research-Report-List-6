<p><p>The Industrial Single-stage Magnetic Pump market can be classified into two types based on the shaft positioning - Stationary Shaft and Rotating Shaft. In Stationary Shaft pumps, the shaft remains fixed while the impeller rotates around it. This design allows for a simpler and more compact pump structure. On the other hand, in Rotating Shaft pumps, the shaft rotates along with the impeller. This design offers better balance and stability, making it suitable for high-pressure applications.</p></p>
