<p><p>Hyperspectral sensors market types are classified based on the spatial swath, which refers to the width of the image captured by the sensor. The 320, 640, 680, and 1280 spatial swath sensors capture images with varying widths, offering different levels of detail and coverage. The 320 spatial swath sensor captures a relatively narrow image, while the 1280 spatial swath sensor captures a wider image. Each type of sensor is suitable for different applications based on the required level of detail and coverage.</p></p>
