<p><p>X-ray inspection systems are vital for material integrity and safety assessments, employing two primary technology types: film-based and digital imaging techniques. Film-based techniques utilize traditional X-ray film to capture images, requiring chemical processing for development. This method is well-established, but it can be time-consuming and less efficient. In contrast, digital imaging techniques use digital sensors to produce immediate, high-resolution images. They offer faster processing, enhanced image quality, and improved data management, making them increasingly favored in various industries.</p></p>
