<p><p>The Railway Vehicle Wheels Market includes two main types of wheels: Rolling Steel Wheel and Cast Steel Wheel. Rolling Steel Wheels are made by rolling a steel slab into a round shape, providing durability and strength to carry heavy loads. On the other hand, Cast Steel Wheels are made by pouring molten steel into a mold, offering a smoother surface and better performance at high speeds. Both types of wheels play a crucial role in the efficient and safe operation of railway vehicles.</p></p>
