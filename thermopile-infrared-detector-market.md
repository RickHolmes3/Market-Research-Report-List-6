<p><strong>The Thermopile Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li><li>SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermopile infrared detector market includes two primary types: through-hole and SMD (Surface Mount Device) thermopile infrared sensors. Through-hole thermopile sensors are designed with leads that pass through mounting holes on a circuit board, suitable for applications requiring robust physical connections. In contrast, SMD thermopile sensors are compact and mounted directly onto the surface of the PCB, enabling high-density designs and automated assembly. Both types are utilized in diverse applications, including temperature measurement, gas detection, and occupancy sensing.</p></p>
