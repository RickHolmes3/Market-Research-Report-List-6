<p><ul><li>Spunbond</li><li>Needlefelt</li><li>Wet laid</li><li>Melt blown</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air filtration media encompasses various types, each tailored for specific applications. Spunbond media is created through spinning continuous filaments, offering strength and durability. Needlefelt is crafted by entangling fibers, providing excellent filtration efficiency and resistance to high temperatures. Wet laid media combines fibers in a slurry, resulting in a highly porous structure suited for fine particulate capture. Melt blown media utilizes thermal processes to produce ultra-fine fibers, making it ideal for capturing smaller particles and aerosols, enhancing air quality.</p></p>
