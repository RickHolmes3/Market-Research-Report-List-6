<p><p>Influenza (Flu) Antiviral Drugs market includes various types such as Penetration and Dehulling Inhibitors, DNA Polymerase Inhibitors, Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors, Protein Inhibitors, Neuraminidase Inhibitors, and Broad-spectrum Antiviral Drugs. Penetration and Dehulling Inhibitors target the entry of the virus into host cells. DNA Polymerase Inhibitors interfere with viral replication by targeting the enzyme responsible for synthesizing viral DNA. Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors target the enzyme responsible for converting viral RNA into DNA. Neuraminidase Inhibitors inhibit the enzyme essential for virus release, while Broad-spectrum Antiviral Drugs target multiple stages of viral replication.</p></p>
