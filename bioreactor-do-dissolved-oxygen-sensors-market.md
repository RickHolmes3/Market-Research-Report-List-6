Type I sensors are typically polarographic or Clark type sensors, which measure DO based on oxygen consumption at a working electrode. On the other hand, Type II sensors are optical sensors that use luminescent technology to measure DO levels. Type II sensors are known for their rapid response time and accurate readings, making them ideal for real-time monitoring and control in bioreactors.</p></p>
