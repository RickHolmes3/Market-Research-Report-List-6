<p><p>Assembly adhesives are a crucial component in various industries for joining materials together. Two common types of assembly adhesives are paste and tape. Paste adhesives are supplied in a thicker consistency and are commonly used for bonding irregular surfaces or filling gaps. Tape adhesives are thin strips with adhesive on one or both sides, providing a convenient and clean application for quick bonding. Both paste and tape adhesives offer strong bonding properties and versatility for a wide range of applications in the market.</p></p>
