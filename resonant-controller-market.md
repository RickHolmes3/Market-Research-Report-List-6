<p><ul><li>Hybrid Flyback</li><li>LLC controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resonant controller market features two prominent types: hybrid flyback and LLC controllers. Hybrid flyback controllers combine traditional flyback topology with resonant techniques, offering improved efficiency and reduced electromagnetic interference. They are ideal for applications requiring compact designs. LLC controllers utilize a half-bridge topology and resonance to achieve high efficiency, particularly in low to medium power ranges. They excel in applications where low switching losses and high power density are essential, making them suitable for various industrial and consumer electronics.</p></p>
