<p><strong>The Lab Grinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Mill</li><li>Disc Mill</li><li>Rotor Mill</li><li>Cutting Mill</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab grinders market includes various types of mills. Ball mills utilize balls for grinding material in a rotating drum, ideal for fine powders. Disc mills use circular plates for shear and impact, effective for brittle materials. Rotor mills employ high-speed rotors to rapidly grind and homogenize samples. Cutting mills operate with sharp blades to slice and reduce soft or fibrous materials. Other types may encompass specialized or hybrid grinding technologies, catering to diverse research and industrial applications.</p></p>
