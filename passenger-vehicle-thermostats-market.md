<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Thermostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insert Thermostat</li><li>Housing Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger vehicle thermostats come in two main types: insert thermostats and housing thermostats. Insert thermostats are installed directly into the engine block, while housing thermostats are integrated into a housing unit. Insert thermostats are more commonly used in older vehicles, while housing thermostats are found in newer models. Both types regulate the engine's temperature by controlling the flow of coolant. Insert thermostats can be easier to replace, while housing thermostats may offer better temperature control due to the integrated design.</p></p>
