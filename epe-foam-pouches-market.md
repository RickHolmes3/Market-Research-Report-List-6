<p><p>EPE foam pouches are categorized based on the density of the polyethylene used in their production. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers durability and strength, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE) combines flexibility with moderate strength, ideal for various packaging needs. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is lightweight and flexible, commonly used for general-purpose applications. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) provides enhanced stretch and tear resistance, making it suitable for more demanding packaging solutions. Each type serves specific market requirements.</p></p>
