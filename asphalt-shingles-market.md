<p><p>The asphalt shingles market is categorized into three main types: high-performance laminated, laminated, and three-tab shingles. High-performance laminated shingles offer superior durability and a layered design for enhanced aesthetics and wind resistance. Laminated shingles, slightly less robust, still provide a stylish look with good performance. Three-tab shingles are the most basic type, featuring a flat design and lower cost, but offer less durability and style compared to the other two types, making them a budget-friendly option.</p></p>
