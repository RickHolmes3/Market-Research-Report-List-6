<p><strong>The PM2.5 Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PM2.5 monitors market includes various technologies, primarily TEOM monitors and beta attenuation monitors. TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitors measure particulate matter by using a tapering element that vibrates, providing real-time mass concentration data. Beta attenuation monitors utilize a radioactive source to measure the attenuation of beta radiation as it passes through airborne particles, offering highly accurate mass readings. Both types play crucial roles in air quality monitoring, supporting regulatory compliance and public health initiatives.</p></p>
