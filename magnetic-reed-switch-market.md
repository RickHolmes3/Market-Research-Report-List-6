<p><p>The magnetic reed switch market is categorized into three main types: Form A, Form B, and Form C switches. Form A switches are normally open, activating when exposed to a magnetic field, ideal for applications requiring on/off control. Form B switches are normally closed, becoming open when a magnetic field is present, suited for safety and security systems. Form C switches are versatile, offering both normally open and normally closed functions, making them applicable in various industrial and consumer electronics.</p></p>
