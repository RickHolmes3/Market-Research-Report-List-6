<p><p>Polypropylene catalyst market mainly consists of two types: Ziegler-Natta Catalyst and Metallocene Catalyst. Ziegler-Natta catalysts are traditional catalysts used for polypropylene production, known for their high activity and selectivity. Whereas, Metallocene catalysts are a newer generation of catalysts that offer improved control over the polymer structure and properties, resulting in higher performance materials. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in the polypropylene industry, catering to different needs and applications.</p></p>
