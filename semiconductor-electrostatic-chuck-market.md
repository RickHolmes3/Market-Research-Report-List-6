<p><strong>The Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor electrostatic chuck market comprises primarily two types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. The Coulomb type uses electrostatic forces to hold semiconductor wafers securely during manufacturing processes, relying on electric fields generated by applied voltage. In contrast, the JR type employs a unique mechanism where electrostatic attraction is enhanced through specially designed electrodes, enabling higher holding forces and better control over wafer positioning. Both types are essential for improving efficiency and precision in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
