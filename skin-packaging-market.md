<p><p>Skin packaging market can be divided into two types: Carded Skin Packaging and Non-Carded Skin Packaging. Carded skin packaging involves placing the product on a pre-formed card before covering it with a clear plastic film. This type of packaging is commonly used for displaying products in retail settings. On the other hand, non-carded skin packaging directly seals the product onto a backing board without the need for a pre-formed card. This type of packaging is often used for irregularly shaped or bulkier items.</p></p>
