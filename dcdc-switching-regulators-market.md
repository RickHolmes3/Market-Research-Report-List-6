<p><p>DC/DC Switching Regulators are electronic devices that convert one DC voltage level to another with high efficiency. There are four main types in the market: Buck, Boost, Buck/Boost, and Invert (negative output). The Buck regulator steps down the input voltage, the Boost regulator steps up the input voltage, the Buck/Boost regulator can either step up or step down the input voltage depending on the application, and the Invert regulator produces a negative output voltage from a positive input voltage. Each type has its own unique applications and advantages.</p></p>
