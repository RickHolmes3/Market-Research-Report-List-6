<p><p>Reinforced Carbon–Carbon (RCC) market types include Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process (LIP). CVD involves depositing carbon onto a substrate through chemical reactions, enhancing material properties for applications like aerospace and defense. In contrast, LIP involves infusing a liquid resin into carbon structures, improving mechanical strength and thermal stability. Both methods cater to specific industry needs, providing tailored solutions for high-performance applications where lightweight, high-strength materials are critical. Growth in aerospace drives demand for these technologies.</p></p>
