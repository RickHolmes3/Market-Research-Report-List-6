<p><p>A Shot Peening Machine is a specialized device used to enhance the mechanical properties of materials through a process called shot peening. This process involves bombarding the surface of components with spherical media, known as shot, to induce compressive residual stresses, improve fatigue resistance, and extend the lifespan of parts. These machines are widely utilized in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where the integrity of components under stress is critical.</p><p>The Shot Peening Machine Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by increasing industrial automation and the demand for high-performance materials.
