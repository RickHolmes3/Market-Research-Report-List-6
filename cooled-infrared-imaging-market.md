<p><strong>The Cooled Infrared Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR</li><li>Mid Wave IR</li><li>Long Wave IR</li><li>Far Wave IR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooled infrared imaging markets are segmented into four types based on wavelength. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) operates from 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, ideal for detecting moisture and minerals. Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, excels in high-temperature detections. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR), between 8 to 14 micrometers, is commonly used for thermal imaging applications. Far Wave Infrared (FWIR), extending beyond 14 micrometers, focuses on research and specialized applications, particularly in atmospheric studies. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
