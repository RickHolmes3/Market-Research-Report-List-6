<p><p>The embedded SIM (eSIM) market can be categorized into two main types: Surface Mount Device (SMD) and System in Package (SiP). SMD eSIMs are directly mounted onto circuit boards, offering compact designs and ease of integration in various devices. In contrast, SiP eSIMs integrate multiple components, such as the eSIM and other functionalities, into a single package, optimizing space and enhancing performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for IoT devices, wearables, and connected technologies, driving their market significance.</p></p>
