<p><p>Industrial explosives market is categorized into three main types: Ammonium Nitrate Explosive, Anfo Explosives, and Other explosives. Ammonium Nitrate Explosive consists of a mixture of ammonium nitrate and diesel fuel, which is widely used in mining and construction industries due to its high energy output. Anfo Explosives, composed of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, are commonly used in blasting and quarrying applications. The Other category includes a variety of explosive types such as dynamite, emulsions, and water gels, each tailored for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
