<p><p>The Flame and Detonation Arrestor market is classified into two primary types: vertical and horizontal arrestors. Vertical flame and detonation arrestors are designed to be installed upright, effectively managing upward traveling flames and pressure waves. In contrast, horizontal arrestors are positioned flat, controlling lateral energy from explosions or flames. Both types enhance safety in industrial applications by preventing the propagation of flames and detonations through vent pipes, protecting equipment and personnel from hazardous incidents.</p></p>
