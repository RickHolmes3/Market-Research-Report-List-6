<p><p>The switchable glass market is categorized into three main types: Electrochromic glass, Thermochromic glass, and Photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass adjusts its tint based on an applied voltage, providing energy efficiency and privacy. Thermochromic glass changes color with temperature variations, helping to regulate indoor climates. Photochromic glass reacts to light exposure, automatically darkening in bright sunlight and returning to clear in low light. Each type offers unique benefits for building design, energy conservation, and occupant comfort.</p></p>
