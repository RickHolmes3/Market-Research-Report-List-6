<p><p>The Epoxy Active Diluent Market is categorized into three main types: Monofunctional, Bifunctional, and Other types. Monofunctional diluents possess a single reactive site, enhancing flexibility and reducing viscosity, ideal for coatings and adhesives. Bifunctional diluents contain two reactive sites, allowing for cross-linking, improving mechanical properties, and heat resistance, suitable for more durable applications. The "Other" category encompasses specialized compounds that provide specific functional benefits, catering to niche applications and varying industry requirements, enhancing the versatility of epoxy formulations.</p></p>
