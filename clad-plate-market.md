<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Clad Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded Clad Plate</li><li>Roll Bonded Clad Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clad plate market features two primary types: explosion bonded clad plates and roll bonded clad plates. Explosion bonded clad plates are created by detonating explosives, resulting in a strong metallurgical bond between layers, ideal for applications needing high resistance to corrosion. On the other hand, roll bonded clad plates use mechanical pressure and heat to achieve bonding, making them suitable for high-volume production and applications requiring flexible dimensional control. Both types enhance material properties, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
