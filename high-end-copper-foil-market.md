<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end copper foil market is primarily segmented into two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, offering excellent flexibility and strength, making it ideal for high-frequency applications like flexible printed circuits. Conversely, electrolytic copper foil is manufactured through electrochemical processes, resulting in a smoother surface and better electrical conductivity, primarily used in applications like lithium-ion batteries and advanced electronic devices. Both types cater to specialized and demanding uses in various industries.</p></p>
