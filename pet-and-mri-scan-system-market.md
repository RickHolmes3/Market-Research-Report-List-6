<p><p>The PET and MRI scan system market consists of two main types: traditional PET-MRI systems and helium-free PET-MRI systems. Traditional systems combine positron emission tomography (PET) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) for enhanced diagnostic imaging, leveraging helium-cooled MRI magnets. Conversely, helium-free systems utilize alternative cooling technologies, eliminating dependency on helium, which addresses supply concerns and reduces operational costs. Both types aim to improve imaging accuracy and patient outcomes while catering to evolving healthcare demands and sustainability considerations.</p></p>
