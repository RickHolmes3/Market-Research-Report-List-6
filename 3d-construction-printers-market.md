<p><p>3D Construction Printers utilize different technologies to create structures. Extrusion printers work by depositing layers of material, usually concrete or plastic, to build up the desired structure. Powder Bonding printers use a binder to bond layers of powdered material together, solidifying them into the final shape. Both of these technologies are commonly used in the construction industry for their ability to create complex and customized structures quickly and efficiently. Each type offers unique advantages and limitations, catering to different construction needs.</p></p>
