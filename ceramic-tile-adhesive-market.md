<p><strong>The Ceramic Tile Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cementitious Adhesive</li><li>Dispersion Adhesive</li><li>Reaction Resin Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic tile adhesive market is divided into three main types: cementitious adhesive, dispersion adhesive, and reaction resin adhesive. Cementitious adhesive is the most common type and is made from a mixture of cement, sand, and additives. Dispersion adhesive is water-based and offers good flexibility and durability. Reaction resin adhesive is a two-component system that cures through a chemical reaction, providing high strength and resistance. Each type of adhesive is suited to different tile materials, installation methods, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
