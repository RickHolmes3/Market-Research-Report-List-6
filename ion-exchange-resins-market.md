There are two main types of ion exchange resins - Anion Exchange Resin and Cation Exchange Resin. Anion exchange resins selectively remove negatively charged ions from solutions, while cation exchange resins remove positively charged ions. These resins are crucial for the removal of contaminants from water and for controlling chemical reactions in industrial processes. The market for ion exchange resins continues to grow due to increasing demand for clean water and efficient chemical processing.</p></p>
