<p><p>Nanofiltration membrane market can be classified into three types: polymeric, inorganic, and hybrid membranes. Polymeric membranes are made from synthetic polymers and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and flexibility. Inorganic membranes are made from non-organic materials such as ceramics or metals, offering high durability and chemical resistance. Hybrid membranes combine the advantages of both polymeric and inorganic membranes, providing improved performance in terms of selectivity and permeability. Each type of membrane offers unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
