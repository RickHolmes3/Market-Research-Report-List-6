<p><strong>The 5G Macro Site Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SA (Stand Alone)</li><li>NSA (Non-Stand Alone)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>5G Macro Site market can be categorized into two main types: SA (Stand Alone) and NSA (Non-Stand Alone). SA refers to a standalone network architecture where both control and user plane functions are integrated, enabling independent deployment and operation of 5G networks. On the other hand, NSA involves a non-standalone architecture where the 5G network relies on existing 4G infrastructure for control functions. Both market types offer different advantages and considerations for operators looking to deploy and expand their 5G networks.</p></p>
