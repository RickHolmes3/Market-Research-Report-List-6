<p><p>The medical disinfection and sterilization market encompasses various technologies essential for ensuring healthcare environments are free of pathogens. Steam sterilizers utilize high-pressure steam to effectively eliminate bacteria, viruses, and spores, making them ideal for heat-resistant instruments. Ethylene oxide sterilizers, on the other hand, employ ethylene oxide gas to sterilize sensitive medical devices that cannot withstand high temperatures. Both methods play crucial roles in infection control, facilitating safe medical procedures and protecting patient safety within healthcare settings.</p></p>
