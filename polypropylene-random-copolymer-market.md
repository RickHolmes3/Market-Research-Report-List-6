<p><p>Polypropylene random copolymer (PP-r) is divided into three main market types: MPP (Medium Performance Polypropylene), MCPP (Modified Crystallinity Polypropylene), and Other. MPP has a balance of good stiffness and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications like automotive parts. MCPP has high impact resistance and transparency, commonly used in household and packaging products. The "Other" market includes specialty PP-r grades tailored for specific applications such as pipes, medical devices, and textiles. Each market type offers unique properties to cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
