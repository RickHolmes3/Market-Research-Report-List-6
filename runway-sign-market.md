Informational runway signs provide pilots with crucial information about the airport layout, such as taxiway names and runway designations. Directional signs, on the other hand, guide pilots on the ground by indicating the direction to specific locations or facilities, such as terminals, hangars, or fueling stations. Both types of runway signs play a vital role in ensuring safe and efficient aircraft operations on the ground.</p></p>
