<p><p>Travelling Wave Tubes (TWTs) are categorized into two main types in the market: Helix TWTs and Coupled Cavity TWTs. Helix TWTs use a helical slow-wave structure to achieve amplification, while Coupled Cavity TWTs use a series of resonant cavities to amplify the signal. Helix TWTs are commonly used in satellite communication and radar systems, while Coupled Cavity TWTs are preferred for high-power applications such as particle accelerators. Both types offer high efficiency and wide bandwidth, making them suitable for various communication and industrial applications.</p></p>
