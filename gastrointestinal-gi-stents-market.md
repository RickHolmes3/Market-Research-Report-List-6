<p><p>The gastrointestinal (GI) stents market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Biliary stents facilitate bile flow by bypassing obstructions in the bile ducts. Esophageal stents alleviate esophageal strictures, improving swallowing. Duodenal stents relieve blockages in the duodenum, enhancing nutrient absorption. Pancreatic stents manage pancreatic duct obstructions, aiding digestion. Colonic stents address colon blockages, often due to tumors, restoring intestinal function. Each type targets specific gastrointestinal disorders, improving patient quality of life and outcomes through minimally invasive interventions.</p></p>
