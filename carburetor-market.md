<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carburetor market consists of two main types: Diaphragm Carburetor and Float-Feed Carburetor. Diaphragm Carburetors use a flexible membrane to regulate the flow of air and fuel into the engine. They are commonly used in small engines such as lawn mowers and chainsaws. Float-Feed Carburetors have a float chamber that regulates the fuel level, ensuring a consistent mixture of air and fuel. They are typically found in larger engines such as motorcycles and cars. Both types play a crucial role in the efficient functioning of internal combustion engines.</p></p>
