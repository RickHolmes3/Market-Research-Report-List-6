<p><p>The neurovascular medical devices market encompasses various types designed for treating cerebral vascular conditions. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to prevent blood flow to aneurysms, while cerebral balloon angioplasty and stenting systems help to open narrowed blood vessels. Support devices assist in maintaining the structural integrity of blood vessels during procedures. Neurothrombectomy devices are utilized for removing blood clots from intracranial vessels, enhancing patient outcomes in conditions like stroke and aneurysms.</p></p>
