<p><strong>The Visible Light Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flash Effect Sensor</li><li>External Photoelectric Effect Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The visible light sensor market includes various types such as flash effect sensors and external photoelectric effect sensors. Flash effect sensors capture brief bursts of light, making them ideal for applications like flash photography and monitoring rapid changes in illumination. In contrast, external photoelectric effect sensors rely on the interaction of light with materials to detect and measure radiation from external sources. These sensors find applications in security systems, automation, and environmental monitoring, enhancing the ability to analyze light conditions effectively.</p></p>
