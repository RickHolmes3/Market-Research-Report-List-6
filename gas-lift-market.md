<p><ul><li>Continuous Flow</li><li>Intermittent Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the gas lift market, two main types are continuous flow and intermittent flow. Continuous flow involves a steady injection of gas into the well to lift the hydrocarbons to the surface consistently. On the other hand, intermittent flow includes the periodic injection of gas in a timed manner to enhance production. Both methods are employed in oil and gas production to optimize well performance and increase recovery rates. Companies choose between these approaches based on well conditions and production requirements.</p></p>
