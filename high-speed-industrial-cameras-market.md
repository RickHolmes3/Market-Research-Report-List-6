<p><p>High Speed Industrial Cameras are divided into two main market types: Line Scan Camera and Area Scan Camera. Line Scan Camera captures images line by line, making it ideal for inspecting continuous objects like moving conveyor belts. Area Scan Camera captures a full image at once, making it suitable for still objects or larger areas. Line Scan Cameras are more commonly used for applications that require precise imaging of moving objects, while Area Scan Cameras are preferred for stationary objects or wide field of view applications.</p></p>
