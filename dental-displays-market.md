<p><p>The dental display market is primarily segmented into LCD and LED technologies. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) screens provide excellent image clarity and are cost-effective, making them popular for dental offices. They are ideal for displaying X-rays and treatment plans. In contrast, LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays offer superior brightness, energy efficiency, and longer lifespans. They enhance visibility and color accuracy, making them suitable for high-definition imaging and presentations in dental practices, promoting better patient engagement and education.</p></p>
