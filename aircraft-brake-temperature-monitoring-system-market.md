<p><ul><li>Non-contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Brake Temperature Monitoring System market is categorized into non-contact and contact types. Non-contact systems utilize infrared sensors to measure the temperature of the brakes from a distance, providing real-time data without physical contact. Conversely, contact systems involve direct thermal sensors attached to the brake components, offering precise temperature readings. Both types are crucial for ensuring optimal braking performance and enhancing safety, as they help prevent overheating and potential brake failures during flight operations.</p></p>
