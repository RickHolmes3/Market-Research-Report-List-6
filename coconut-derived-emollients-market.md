<p><p>Coconut-derived emollients are categorized into three main types: humectants, occlusives, and lubricants. Humectants, like glycerin, attract moisture from the environment to hydrate the skin. Occlusives, such as coconut oil, create a barrier that locks in moisture, preventing water loss. Lubricants provide a smooth application, enhancing texture and glide in skincare products. Together, these emollients improve skin hydration, texture, and overall health, driving demand in the cosmetic and personal care markets.</p></p>
