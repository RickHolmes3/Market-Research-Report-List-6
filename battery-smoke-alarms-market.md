<p><p>The battery smoke alarms market features three primary types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric Type**: These alarms use a light source to detect smoke particles, offering faster response times to smoldering fires, making them ideal for detecting slow-burning fires.</p><p>2. **Ionization Type**: Employing radioactive material to sense changes in air ionization, these alarms are more effective for quick-flaming fires but can be less reliable for smoldering fires.</p><p>3. **Others**: This category includes specialized alarms, such as combination units that incorporate both technologies or smart smoke alarms with additional features like connectivity and alerts.</p></p>
